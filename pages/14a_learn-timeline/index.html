Learn gsap.timeline()

The gsap.timeline() method is where GSAP truly shines. It allows you to create complex animation sequences by chaining multiple animations together. This gives you precise control over the timing, order, and synchronization of your animations.

Think of a timeline as a container for your animations. You can add animations to the timeline, and they will play in sequence. You can also overlap animations, create complex timings, and even nest timelines within other timelines.
