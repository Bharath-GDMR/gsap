Understanding Properties

GSAP can animate any numeric value, but its real power lies in its ability to understand and manipulate a wide range of properties. From simple CSS properties to complex transforms and colors, GSAP handles them all with ease.

In this lesson, you'll learn about the different types of properties you can animate and how GSAP interprets them. This knowledge is fundamental to creating sophisticated and visually rich animations.

For a comprehensive list of animatable properties, refer to the Official GSAP docs.
