Back Easing

Back easing adds a playful and energetic quality to your animations. It works by having the animation overshoot its target before settling into its final position, creating a fun, elastic effect.

You can control the amount of overshoot by adjusting the ease's configuration. This makes it perfect for creating eye-catching and dynamic user interfaces.
