Power Easing

Power easing creates a more dramatic effect by accelerating and decelerating with more force. It's perfect for creating animations that feel snappy and responsive.

There are four main types of power easing: power1, power2, power3, and power4, each with increasing intensity. You can also use easeIn, easeOut, and easeInOut to control the timing of the ease.
